Beauty Care

At Mea’s Aesthetics, beauty goes beyond the lash table — we care about the health of your natural lashes and the quality of your hair. From precise lash applications to expert hair revamps, we prioritize clean techniques and long-lasting results.

✨ Aftercare Tips:

  • Avoid water, steam, and oil-based products for the first 24 hours.
  • Wrap your hair at night to maintain its sleek look
  • For hair: use sulfate-free products to extend the life of your revamp.
  • Schedule infills every 2–3 weeks for best retention.
