Beauty Care

At Mea’s Aesthetics, beauty goes beyond the lash table — it’s about preserving the health of your natural lashes. We emphasize proper application, gentle handling, and clean products for stunning results that last.

✨ Aftercare Tips:

  • Avoid water, steam, and oil-based products for the first 24 hours.
  • Brush your lashes daily to keep them neat.
  • Don’t rub your eyes or sleep on your face.
  • Schedule infills every 2–3 weeks for best retention.
