About Us

At Mea’s Aesthetics, beauty is more than a look — it’s an experience. While we’re known for our luxury lash work, we also specialize in revamping hair to restore confidence and elevate your style.

From nourishing treatments to silk press and trims, our hair care services are designed to repair damage, bring out shine, and leave you feeling refreshed. Healthy, beautiful hair is always in fashion — and we’re here to help you achieve it.
